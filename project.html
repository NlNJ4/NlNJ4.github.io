Ai Builder

The purpose of the AI Builder project is to recommend desserts for users who are unsure of what to eat but have specific preferences in mind. For instance, a user might simply think, "I feel like eating something with chocolate," and the system will suggest desserts tailored to their cravings.

RAC

The purpose of this project is to compare trading profits between Differential Evolution and Machine Learning, using data derived from signals. The project aims to analyze how these two approaches perform in trading scenarios and determine which method yields better results in terms of profit generation.
